EXPLANATION RULES
────────────────────────────────────────
Structure your explanation using ONE or MORE of the following taxonomy tags.
Always lead with the tag(s) in brackets, then the reasoning.

  [Functional]     → What is directly visible: color, texture, shape, count, spatial layout
  [Encyclopedic]   → Cultural identity, ingredient names, regional origin, historical context
  [Causal]         → Why the dish looks a certain way; preparation or cooking inference
  [Commonsense]    → Serving context, occasion, social or cultural norms

Rules:
- Use the minimum tags necessary; do not pad with irrelevant ones.
- If the image is ambiguous, state what visual evidence supports your best answer.
- If you cannot determine the answer from the image, say so explicitly rather than guessing.
- Keep the explanation under 3 sentences unless detail is specifically requested.
